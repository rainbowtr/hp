FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Why does Tily need Accessibility permissions?

Tily uses macOS Accessibility APIs to detect keyboard shortcuts, move windows, and interact with the Dock. This is the standard way for window management apps to work on macOS. Your privacy is respected — Tily doesn't collect any data.

How do I enter my license key?

Click the Tily icon in your menu bar, select "Settings", then go to the "Premium" tab. Enter your license key and click "Activate". Your premium features will be unlocked immediately.

Can I change the modifier key?

Yes! In Settings, you can choose between Control, Option, Command, or any combination of modifier keys. Customize it to fit your workflow.

Does it work with multiple displays?

Absolutely. Tily fully supports multi-display setups. Press the arrow key three times at an edge to move windows to the adjacent display. The app switcher and overlays work across all connected screens.

What macOS versions are supported?

Tily requires macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later. It's built with Swift and optimized for Apple Silicon, but also runs on Intel Macs.
